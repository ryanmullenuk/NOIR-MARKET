NOIR MARKET V1.4 CHANGELOG

Baseline
- Built from Noir Market V1.3.
- Existing V1.3 features preserved unless directly updated by this release.

Versioning
- Updated visible title, manifest version, service worker cache and save version to V1.4.
- V1.4 saves use noir_market_v1_4.
- Load migration retained for V1.3, V1.2 and older known save keys.

UI
- News ticker text is now uppercase.
- Replaced the old marquee ticker with a CSS-based smooth scrolling ticker.
- Notifications such as LOAN ACCEPTED and INSUFFICIENT FUNDS are forced into a top-layer display so they remain visible above modals and other screens.
- Storage and vault action buttons have been reworked into grouped rows to prevent button overlap and cramped text.

News and Economy
- Daily news now includes the main market event plus two additional random stories.
- Added random increased-police-presence stories affecting a random city.
- Added random spiked-batch stories affecting a different/random city and drug.
- News stories continue to affect city-level prices, supply, heat and market trends.

Hustle
- Rebuilt Hustle with three informants:
  - Simon the Snitch: £100, low trust.
  - Loose Lisa: £250, medium trust.
  - Pistol Pete: £10,000, high trust.
- Each informant has a short criminal-style bio.
- Any informant has a 10% chance to scam the player and run.
- Added Businesses section marked COMING SOON.

Audio
- Added the uploaded MP3 music file.
- Background music now points to assets/music/noir_theme.mp3.
- Existing WAV remains in the package as a fallback asset.

Preserved Features
- Splash screen logo.
- Dust particle effects.
- Background music behaviour.
- Shady lenders.
- City vault system and vault upgrades.
- Flight seizure warnings.
- Sell All buttons.
- Storage/Vault transfer system.
- Random police raids.
- Stash discoveries.
- Supplier arrests.
- Festival demand spikes.
- Customs seizures.
- Rodent events.
- Weapon seizure mechanics.
