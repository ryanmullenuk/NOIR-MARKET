NOIR MARKET V2.0

Bug fix and UI release built from V1.9.

Changes:
- Replaced the remaining marquee-based news ticker with a clipped ticker track.
- News ticker now scrolls across the full available news bar area without overlapping the NEWS label.
- Added measured ticker animation so scrolling remains smooth across different text lengths and screen sizes.
- Storage vault upgrade button is now smaller, amber, and aligned to the right of the storage panel.
- Travel / Shipping export destinations are now displayed as selectable tiles instead of a dropdown list.
- Shipping export behaviour, costs and shipment logic remain unchanged.
- Updated app title, manifest version, service worker cache and save version to V2.0.
- V1.9 and older save migration retained.
