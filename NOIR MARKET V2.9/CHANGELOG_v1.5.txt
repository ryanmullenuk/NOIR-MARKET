NOIR MARKET V1.5 CHANGELOG

Release focus: proportional loan interest and early repayment behaviour.

Changes made:
- Loans now store principal, interest rate, start day, due day and term length.
- Full advertised interest is only charged when the player reaches the full loan term.
- Early repayment now charges a proportional amount of interest based on days elapsed.
- Same-day repayment carries a 10% minimum interest charge on the full interest amount to prevent risk-free borrowing.
- Loan screens now show the current settlement figure and the full-term repayment figure.
- Bank loan list now labels full-term interest clearly.
- Debt due screen now uses the current live settlement value.
- Missed payments now convert the current settlement value plus a 25% penalty into a new one-day rollover debt.
- Removed the old daily 2% automatic debt increase from day progression.
- General debt payment now pays down live loan balances instead of only reducing a detached debt number.
- V1.4, V1.3, V1.2 and older save loading remains supported.
- Legacy loans that only stored a fixed repay amount are migrated safely as fixed-value loans.
