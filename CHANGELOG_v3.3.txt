NOIR MARKET V3.3 CHANGELOG

Release type: Feature and splash polish release
Baseline: Noir Market V3.2

Changes made:
- Updated release version to V3.3.
- Changed splash snow so it starts immediately from the top of the screen.
- Kept the splash screen locked full screen with clipped canvas edges to avoid visible snow streaks around the border.
- Added 20 new random price events: 10 negative supply/risk/quality events and 10 positive supply/glut/heat reduction events.
- Added short-lived price modifiers with duration handling for event-driven price movement.
- Added city-specific news ticker stories for London, Manchester, Birmingham, Liverpool, Bristol, Glasgow, Cardiff and Leeds.
- Updated news generation so ticker lines feel sharper, darker, more British and less generic.
- Added random timed informant offer popups during active play.
- Added PAY and SLAP AWAY informant choices.
- Added Simon the Snitch (£100), Loose Lisa (£250) and Pistol Pete (£10,000) to timed popups.
- Added hidden informant outcome split: real tip, false tip, scam, or vague harmless tip.
- Added affordability handling when the player cannot pay for a tip.
- Added reputation effects for slapping away Lisa/Pete and being scammed by Lisa/Pete.
- Prevented informant popups from stacking or triggering during splash, inactive play or other modals.
- Retained MP3-free build.
- Retained existing gameplay systems, travel, shipping, market, storage, loans, quick navigation and save migration.
