Noir Market V3.2 Changelog

- Incremented release version from V3.1 to V3.2.
- Added a splash-only canvas snow/parallax overlay adapted from the requested CodePen effect.
- Added depth-based particle sizing, opacity, fall speed, sway and pointer-influenced wind.
- Kept the effect limited to the splash screen only and stops it after entering the game.
- Disabled the old DOM live-dust layer on the splash to avoid duplicate particle systems.
- Kept the larger ENTER text on the loading bar.
- Updated service worker cache name and manifest version to V3.2.
- Preserved gameplay systems and save migration from V3.1 and older versions.
